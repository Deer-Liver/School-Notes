Pythagorean therom:

Formula: a² + b² = c²

Cases:
    1. If both legs (Shorter sides) are known use the above Formula

    2. If one leg and the hypotonouse is known, use the fallowing formulas
        A. If side A is unkown a² = c² – b²
        B. If side B is unkown b² = c² – a²

Examples:
    1. c² = 4² + 3²
        A. c² = 16 + 9
        B. c² = 25
        C. c² = √25
        D. C = 5
    2. a² = 5² – 3²
        A. a² = 25 – 9
        B. a² = 16
        C. a = √16
        D. a = 4
